Privacy Policy

Your home data belongs to you.

Home Floorplan is designed around a simple principle: the information that describes your home should stay under your control.

01

Local first

The product is being designed to keep core floorplan, room and accessory context on device whenever possible.

02

Your control

When cloud or AI features are used, the goal is to make sharing explicit, limited and understandable.

03

No advertising profile

Home Floorplan is not built to sell ads or create advertising profiles from your home data.

Current policy status

Home Floorplan is not yet publicly distributed. This page defines the intended privacy posture for the beta and will be updated before TestFlight or App Store distribution.

Data categories

The app may process floorplan layouts, room names, accessory placement, environmental readings, security states, habits and diagnostic information needed to make the experience work.

AI features

AI features are intended to use the minimum context necessary to generate useful suggestions. The product should clearly indicate when information is processed outside the device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@gethomefloorplan.app.
