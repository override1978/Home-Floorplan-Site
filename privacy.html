Overview

Home Floorplan helps you view, organize, and control compatible Apple Home/HomeKit accessories using a visual floorplan and smart home tools.

Effective date: [DATE]

Data the app may access

Depending on the permissions and features you choose to use, Home Floorplan may access:

  • Apple Home/HomeKit data, including homes, rooms, accessories, services, scenes, automations, accessory states, and sensor values.
  • Floorplan data you create or import, including placed markers and room/accessory layout information.
  • Environmental sensor readings from compatible HomeKit accessories, such as temperature, humidity, air quality, occupancy, light, or similar sensor data.
  • Location information when you grant location permission, used for home context, weather/context features, map selection, and geofence setup where available.
  • Photos or files you select when importing a floorplan image.
  • Camera or RoomPlan data when you choose to scan or create a floorplan.
  • Microphone and speech input only when you choose to use voice features.
  • Notification preferences and local app settings.
  • Optional AI prompts and related home context when you choose to configure and use an AI provider.

How data is used

Home control

Display and control HomeKit accessories, rooms, scenes, and automations.

Floorplans

Save your floorplan, markers, accessory positions, and local preferences.

Insights

Generate local summaries, environmental context, and optional AI-assisted suggestions.

Local storage

Home Floorplan stores app data locally on your device, including floorplans, preferences, selected settings, activity history, and generated insights.

Deleting the app from the device may delete locally stored app data. Installing an update over an existing installation normally keeps local app data, subject to iOS behavior.

AI providers

AI features are optional. If you configure an AI provider or API key, Home Floorplan may send selected prompts, home context, room names, accessory information, sensor summaries, and relevant action context to that provider to generate responses or suggestions.

Do not enable AI features if you do not want this information processed by the selected provider.

Home Floorplan does not bundle your personal AI API key in the app. If API keys are used, they should be provided by you and stored securely on device.

Apple Home and HomeKit

HomeKit access is required for core smart home functionality. Home Floorplan reads HomeKit data only after you grant permission through iOS.

HomeKit automations created or edited by the app are stored and executed through Apple Home/HomeKit.

Permissions

Location

If you grant location permission, Home Floorplan may use your current location for home context, weather/context features, map selection, or geofence setup. The app does not require Always location permission for the first release unless explicitly shown by iOS in a future version.

Photos, camera, microphone, and speech

Home Floorplan accesses photos, files, camera, microphone, or speech recognition only when you use features that require them, such as importing a floorplan, scanning a room, or using voice input.

Tracking and data sharing

Home Floorplan does not use advertising tracking in the current release.

Home Floorplan does not sell your data.

Data may be processed by Apple services required for HomeKit, iCloud/device platform functionality, TestFlight, App Store distribution, or crash reporting; optional AI providers you configure and choose to use; and other service providers only if added and disclosed in a future version.

Retention and deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, until the app prunes older records where applicable, or until you delete the app.

To remove app data, delete data from the app where available or delete the app from your device. HomeKit accessories, scenes, and automations managed by Apple Home may need to be removed from Apple Home separately.
