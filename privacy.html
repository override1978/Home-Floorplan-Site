Privacy Policy

Your home data belongs to you.

Last updated: June 29, 2026. Home Floorplan is designed to keep the information that describes your home under your control, on your iPad first.

01

On-device first

Habits, usage insights, environmental suggestions and your floorplan are processed on your iPad. They are not sent to me.

02

No advertising profile

Your home data is never used for advertising, marketing, profiling or use-based data mining.

03

No account required

Home Floorplan does not require a login and does not track you across apps or websites.

Home Floorplan is built and maintained by Maurizio Cinti, an independent developer based in Italy. This policy explains what the app does with your data, and what it deliberately does not do.

The short version: I do not run a server that collects your home activity, I do not see your home data, and I do not use it for advertising or profiling.

Controller

Who is responsible

The data controller for GDPR purposes is Maurizio Cinti, independent developer based in Italy.

For any privacy question, or to exercise a right described below, write to hello@gethomefloorplan.app. One person reads everything that comes in.

Apple Home

HomeKit access

With your permission, granted through the iPadOS system prompt, Home Floorplan reads information from Apple Home: home, room and accessory names, accessory placement, current accessory state and sensor readings such as temperature, humidity, air quality, light, motion and contact sensors.

The app can also send commands to your accessories when you control them from the floorplan. You can review or revoke this access at any time in Settings -> Privacy & Security -> HomeKit.

Legal basis: consent through the system permission, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR, and performance of the service you requested, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

On-device intelligence

History, habits and floorplans

To recognise patterns over time, the app keeps a rolling history of accessory events and sensor readings on your iPad. From that history it computes habits, behavioural patterns, usage anomalies and environmental suggestions entirely on device.

Your floorplan, rooms, layout, furniture, accessory markers and any image you import to trace over are also stored on your device. None of this is transmitted to me.

Legal basis: performance of the service you requested, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR, and legitimate interest in providing the app's core features, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Weather

Apple WeatherKit

To show outdoor conditions next to your rooms, the app may request weather data from Apple WeatherKit using your home's approximate coordinates. The app does not use continuous or background location tracking and does not track your or your family's location.

This request is handled by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Legal basis: legitimate interest in providing the weather feature, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Optional AI

AI features are off by default

Home Floorplan can use an AI model to add plain-language names or short explanations on top of patterns your iPad has already detected. These features are optional.

  • You provide your own API key for the AI provider you choose. The key is stored securely in the iPad Keychain.
  • When an AI feature runs, the app sends a small, pre-aggregated summary directly from your device to that provider.
  • It does not send raw event history and it does not pass through a Home Floorplan server.

Your use of that provider is governed by your own agreement with them, including their privacy policy, for example Anthropic or OpenAI. You can turn AI features off or remove your API key at any time.

Legal basis: consent, given by enabling the feature, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

Beta and store

TestFlight and App Store data

While the app is distributed through TestFlight, Apple may collect standard diagnostics such as crash logs and basic usage information to help improve app quality. Feedback you submit through TestFlight is shared with me and may include a screenshot, device model and iPadOS version if you choose to send it.

On the App Store, Apple may provide aggregated and anonymised statistics. This is handled under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Legal basis: legitimate interest in maintaining and improving the app, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Limits

What I do not do

  • I do not sell or rent your data.
  • I do not use home data for advertising, marketing, profiling or use-based data mining.
  • I do not show ads in the app.
  • I do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs to track your behaviour.
  • I do not require an account and I do not build a profile of you.

Retention

How long data is kept

Detailed history, such as individual accessory events and sensor readings, is kept on your device on a rolling basis of about 30 days. After that, it is automatically removed, while condensed summaries may be kept so long-term insights continue to work.

Your floorplan and approved automations stay until you change or remove them.

Control

How to delete or stop processing

  • Delete the app to remove locally stored data from your device.
  • Revoke HomeKit access in iPadOS Settings to stop the app reading your home data.
  • Turn off AI features or remove your API key to stop AI-related requests.

Rights

Your rights

Under the GDPR and similar laws, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

Because Home Floorplan processes your data on your device and I do not collect it centrally, you can exercise most of these rights directly by viewing, editing or deleting data in the app, revoking permissions or removing the app. For anything else, write to hello@gethomefloorplan.app.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority. In Italy, this is the Garante per la protezione dei dati personali.

Other notes

Children, transfers and changes

Home Floorplan is not directed at children and is not intended for use by children. I do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your home data is processed on your device and is not transferred by me. If you enable optional AI features, summaries go to the provider you choose, which may process them outside the EU/EEA under your agreement with that provider. Apple services such as WeatherKit are operated by Apple under its own safeguards.

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, I will update this page and the date above before the change takes effect.
