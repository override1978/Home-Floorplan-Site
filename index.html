See your home differently.

Home Floorplan shows rooms, accessories, and sensors in one spatial view.

A home never stands still.

People come and go, the temperature rises, light shifts through the day, and small habits repeat.

Home Floorplan puts all of it on your floorplan, so you can see it happening where it belongs, not on just another screen.

See

Chapter 01

Everything where you expect it.

Draw the floorplan for each floor and find lights, sensors, and accessories exactly where they are in real life. No lists to scroll through: tap a room and control what's inside.

Feel

Chapter 02

Understand the air before you even walk in.

Temperature, humidity, and air quality for each room, readable at a glance on the floorplan. You can see right away if the bedroom is too dry or if the kitchen needs a window open, without jumping through ten screens.

Learn

Chapter 03

Learn how your home really lives.

Day by day, Home Floorplan notices the things you do at the same time, or in the same way, and shows them to you. When a habit is clear enough, it suggests turning it into an automation. Everything stays on your iPad, nothing goes to the cloud.

Create

Chapter 04

Make it live on the wall.

A fixed iPad in the hallway or kitchen, always on, showing your home instead of a grid of icons. Home Floorplan was made to live there.
